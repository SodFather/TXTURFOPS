Core Service

Weed Control

Our weed control program uses a two-pronged approach to keep your lawn weed-free year-round:

Pre-Emergent

Applied in late winter and early spring (Rounds 1 & 2) to create a barrier that prevents weed seeds from germinating. Products like Barricade and Dimension stop crabgrass, dandelions, and other weeds before they appear.

Post-Emergent

Targeted spot treatments for weeds that break through, active March through November. We use selective herbicides like Celsius, MSM Turf, and Certainty that kill weeds without harming your Bermuda or St. Augustine turf.

Common weeds we treat: crabgrass, dandelions, clover, nutsedge, dallisgrass, henbit, chickweed, spurge.

Core Service

Fertilization

Central Texas lawns face unique challenges: alkaline clay soils, intense summer heat, and limestone-based water. Our fertilization programs are formulated specifically for these conditions.

  • Spring feeding (Mar-May) — Jumpstart green-up with slow-release nitrogen
  • Summer nutrition (Jun-Jul) — Iron and micronutrients for color without burn
  • Fall program (Sep-Oct) — Potassium-heavy feed for root development
  • Winterizer (Nov-Dec) — Final feeding to strengthen roots for dormancy

We fertilize Bermuda, St. Augustine, Zoysia, and Buffalo grass. Products and timing adjusted for your specific turf type.

Pest & Grub Control

Lawn-damaging insects are a year-round threat in Central Texas, with peak activity from April through October. We offer both preventive and curative treatments.

Grub Preventive

Applied May-June before grub eggs hatch. Imidacloprid-based treatment provides season-long protection against white grubs that destroy roots.

Surface Pest Control

Bifenthrin-based treatments for fire ants, chinch bugs, armyworms, and other surface feeders. Applied as-needed or preventively.
